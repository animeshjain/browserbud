What can BrowserBud do?

BrowserBud already knows which video you're watching. Just ask.

Ask about the video

"Summarize this video", "What are the key points?" - it pulls the transcript and answers without you having to specify which video.

Ask about this moment

It knows where you are in the video. "What was the speaker trying to say here?" works - it grabs the transcript around your current timestamp.

Capture a frame

Click the camera icon on the top-right of the video player. It captures the current frame and puts it in context so you can ask questions about what's on screen.

Analyze comments

Ask about the comment section - get a summary of reactions, find counterpoints, or see what people disagree on.
